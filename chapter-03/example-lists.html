Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Ingredients

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omellete)
  4. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
